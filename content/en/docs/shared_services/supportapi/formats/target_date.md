Applicable when the case is a business service request. See [CaseType](/docs/shared_services/supportapi/formats/case_type).

* **Data Type**: string
* **Format**: `full-date` utilizing the `date-fullyear "-" date-month "-" date-mday` definition.
* **Example**: `2024-08-20`
